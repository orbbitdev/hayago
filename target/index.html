Our Goal for PKM KC 2023, Indonesia

PKM-KC 2023: A Platform for Students to Transform Constructive Ideas into Reality. Based on creativity and logic, even if it's yet to provide perfect functionality or immediate benefits to others, it's a step in the right direction towards innovation and progress.

We possess an inspiring vision

To engineer autonomous copters that provide innovative, swift, and eco-friendly logistics solutions. We harness advanced intelligent control systems and predictive control estimation to optimize system efficiency and reliability

To develop an autonomous drone delivery system by integrating Sliding Mode Control technology to enhance drone stability and control, alongside the Kalman Filter to ensure accurate navigation and tracking.

3

To provide reliable and prompt delivery services that meet customer needs by offering accessible, affordable, and flexible drone delivery solutions."
